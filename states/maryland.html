Red Lobster Happy Hour in Maryland

Maryland is famous for its blue crabs, but you can still enjoy Red Lobster's Happy Hour specials on shrimp, lobster, and more! From 3-6 PM daily at participating locations, enjoy $5 drinks and $2 off appetizers. With locations throughout the state, from Baltimore to the Eastern Shore, there's always a Red Lobster nearby ready to serve you the freshest seafood.
